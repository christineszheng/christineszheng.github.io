Educator Credentials Content Page

Hi! I’m Christine! I graduated class of 2020 from New York University where I got my Bachelor of Science in math education. After graduation, I got my New York State teaching certification for mathematics 7-12 with grades 5-6 extension. Over the past 3 years, I’ve dedicated myself to teaching algebra at New Visions Charter High School for Advanced Math and Sciences in Brooklyn, NY.
I cater to a diverse student population including English language learners and students with learning disabilities. I mainly specialize in teaching algebra 1 and algebra 2 where I’ve successfully led various work such as an SAT program and the regents preparatory program for the algebra 1, geometry, and algebra 2 regents. Whether it is online or in person settings, I’ve fostered inclusive and effective learning environments. I am passionate about teaching math and I strive to make a positive impact on all students.
